Our Mission

Tech By Choice’s goal is to increase the diversity of the tech industry by offering low to no cost tech skill building events, workshops, and classes to anyone that identifies as a person of color, woman, LGTBQ+, differently abled, or those aligned with the space we're creating.

Creating a space with protected groups as our first priority will empower individuals to enter the tech industry, whether it be through a technical, or non technical roles, consequently increasing tech diversity and improving those individuals careers & lifestyles.
